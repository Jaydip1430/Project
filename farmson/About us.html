About Farmson

Since 2003, Farmson Engineers has been achieving breakthrough quality in the manufacture of hard chrome plated bars and shafts. We are known for our variety, customized manufacturing and delivery according to the industry requirements, and we have now built an ever expanding client base in the country. Our state-of-the-art production facility, equipped with the latest machinery and inhouse processes like bar pilling, straightening, induction hardening, grinding, polishing, hardchrome plating and finishing, we are now perched to expand into newer areas in the market, guided by the insights of a professional management.
