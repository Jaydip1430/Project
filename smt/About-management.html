The Surendranagar Law Society started in 1986 for the purpose of education, which is helpful in self-employment. First Law society started Law College, in co-education, so that one can became self dependent, after one year Law Society started Mahila Home Science College, because being backward area, parents donâ€™t allow their daughters to study in Co-education College.
